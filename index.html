Who We Are

Where ancient myth meets modern chemistry

Artchemys was born from a conviction that science should serve culture. We are a chemistry company building a portfolio of innovative materials — starting with the conservation of art, growing into the future of sustainable chemistry.

Our name carries the hidden spirit of Artemis — Greek goddess of precision and protection — and the aletheia: the uncovering of what is concealed. Like our organogel that reveals the true colors of paintings hidden beneath layers of wax.

Our Technology

eLoZelo™ Organogel

01
pNIPA-LAP Nanocomposite Organogel
Poly(N-isopropylacrylamide) crosslinked with Laponite XLS nanoclay, saturated with our proprietary POA solvent mixture. The world's first safe, effective alternative to TCE for wax-resin lining removal.
Patent Protected
02
Reusable — Minimum 9× Applications
Unlike single-use chemical treatments, eLoZelo can be regenerated and reused at least 9 times without loss of efficacy. Simply immerse in fresh POA solvent — the gel recovers its full cleaning power.
Sustainable
03
Safe for Canvas, Paint & Conservators
Confirmed by FTIR and GC-MS analysis — eLoZelo does not damage linen canvas or polymerized oil paint layers. No special ventilation required. No carcinogenic substances. No hazardous waste disposal.
Laboratory Verified
