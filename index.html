Who We Are

Where ancient myth meets modern chemistry

Artchemys was born from a conviction that science should serve culture. We are a chemistry company building a portfolio of innovative materials — starting with the conservation of art, growing into the future of sustainable chemistry.

Our name carries the hidden spirit of Artemis — Greek goddess of precision and protection — and the aletheia: the uncovering of what is concealed. Like our organogel that reveals the true colors of paintings hidden beneath layers of wax.
